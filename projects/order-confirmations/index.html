Order Confirmations

Local Trash Hauling is a SoCal dumpster rental and junk removal company. When booking jobs they needed a way for customers to agree to their terms and conditions. Rather than using the old approach of signing on-site, this web app allowed customers to review and agree well before the drivers were even on their way. The site allowed for the LTH team to send out and manage all of their customers info in a convinent and user friendly space with a scaleable architecture that could handle such high traffic.
